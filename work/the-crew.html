The Crew 2 UI Redesign

A simple & fun test project. Based on the wireframes provided, i had to create a menu screen for the 'Roster'. This screen allows the player to interacte with others. I had to take the actual style guide in consideration and the overall layout structure.
Link to the Figma prototype

The screens should display:
- Players profile pictures,
- Their progression level,
- See their current activity,
- See their relations with the player,
- See their status, available or offline,
- Visit the players' cards.
