Musée de la Chasse

For this personal project, we imagined a dynamic & modular identity based on the theme "Imaginary Bestiary", to question the relation between humans & animals. We wanted to create something traditionnal & modern, based on modern art & anthropology. We based our identity around shapes and we randomly added some elements, animals or humans characteristics. So we can have a playful & animated logo.
