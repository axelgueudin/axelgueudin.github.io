description: |
 Dark Star is a UI Challenge proposed by Henry Turner, for the WeCanFixItinUI discord. 
 
 The  challenge is to create a fictional UI, for a sci-fi horror game set aboard "The Somerville Expedition," a research vessel facing an alien threat in deep space. 
 
 As the protagonist, navigate through a hostile derelict, shooting, burning, and electrocuting your way to escape while unraveling the mystery.
